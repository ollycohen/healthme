Why HealthMe?

We designed HealthMe because we were tired of outdated, unintuitive, and underdeveloped tooling for something as important as our health. Instead of having seperate tools for tracking nutrition, fitness, and visualizing this data, we combined all three functions into one so that our users could have the most streamlined health optimization experience.
